Trinh Ha Lan Phuong
Lan Phuong was born in Hanoi, Vietnam and has been studying Graphic Design at FPT-Arena Multimedia school. FPT-Arena Multimedia is a training school belonging to FPT corporation in association with the global IT training group Aptech. Established in July 2004, FPT-Arena is the first school to train Multimedia Fine Arts and also the first unit to implement the AMSP program of Arena India in Vietnam.

Lan Phuong is an on-the-study multidisciplinary designer whose work encompasses brand identity, publications, packaging, advertising, and websites..

Phuong has received several design honors. Her work is one of 12 most impressive artworks, which are selected to be printed on postcard 2020 of FPT School by brilliant, talented and dedicated lecturers, who have with deep knowledge of aesthetic value. She also has 2 artworks chosen to be exhibited on 94 Showcase in January 2020 which was organized by FPT Arena Multimedia School.

Her work and critical writing has appeared in FPT Arena Multimedia website. The article has more than 1000 views.
